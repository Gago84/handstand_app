Handstand Free Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

Handstand Free provides training plans and instructional videos for handstand practice. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information handled by the app

The app does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, address, phone number, or email address. Your onboarding choices, training preferences, and premium access flag are stored locally on your device.

Firebase services

The app uses Google Firebase to load training plans and instructional videos. Firebase may process technical information needed to deliver these services, such as network requests and device-related diagnostics, according to Google's privacy terms.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. The app does not receive or store your payment card details.

Video caching

Instructional videos may be downloaded and cached locally on your device to improve playback. You can remove cached data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage where supported.
